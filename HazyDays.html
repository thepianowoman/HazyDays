Exploring Hazy Days


Hazy Days is a unique video game designed to highlight the dangers of air pollution.

Players guide Xiao Feng, a young girl, through her daily life in a polluted city.

By controlling her breathing, the game illustrates the difficulty of staying healthy in smog-filled environments.

The game combines simple mechanics with a powerful message about the human impact of air pollution.

It offers an emotional and eye-opening experience that raises awareness about this global issue.

Created by Mike Ren, Hazy Days is free to play and available for PC and Mac.
